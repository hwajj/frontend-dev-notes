## 4/17 금

I missed my morning workout today. Honestly, it’s kind of on me. I called my boyfriend before bed last night. I was just gonna hear his voice real quick and say, “okay, I’m going to sleep,” but we ended up talking for over an hour. And yeah… that woke me up. I went to bed late, knocked out, and by the time I woke up, my workout time was already gone.

We’re actually not seeing each other right now. That was my decision. He did something pretty serious, so I told him we shouldn’t meet until he starts therapy. I told him to book it ASAP, but he said he’d go once his crypto goes back up. So I was like, “when is that?” and he said “in two months.” So I said, fine—then let’s not see each other for two months. And he agreed. It’s been 10 days. We don’t talk every day, and I even had a whole weekend completely to myself. It actually felt kinda nice. Like… freedom.

I was so mad at him. But I guess feelings don’t just disappear like that. Somehow I already feel less angry. Last night, I caught myself laughing at the dumb things he says again. He’s so dumb. In a cute way. But seriously, why does he sometimes turn into a completely different person? If it weren’t for that side of him… he’d honestly be so easy to love.

오늘 아침 운동 못 갔어. 솔직히 이건 좀 내 탓이야. 어제 자기 전에 남자친구한테 전화했어. 그냥 잠깐 목소리만 듣고 “나 잘게” 하려고 했는데, 근데 결국 한 시간 넘게 통화했어. 그리고… 그게 나를 깨워버렸어. 늦게 자서 그대로 푹 자버렸고, 일어났을 땐 이미 운동 시간이 지나 있었어.

우리 지금 안 만나고 있는 중이야. 그건 내가 정한 거야. 걔가 꽤 큰 잘못을 했어. 그래서 상담 시작하기 전까지 만나지 말자고 했어. 당장 예약하라고 했는데, 근데 코인 오르면 간대. 그래서 내가 “그게 언제인데?” 했지. 걔는 “두 달 뒤”래. 그래서 “그래, 그럼 두 달 보지 말자” 했어. 걔도 동의했고. 10일 됐어. 매일 연락하지도 않고, 주말 통째로 완전히 내 시간으로 보냈어. 근데 좀 좋더라. 약간… 자유 느낌.

진짜 많이 화났었는데. 근데 감정이라는 게 그렇게 쉽게 사라지진 않나 봐. 어느새 화가 좀 풀렸어. 어제는 또 걔 말에 웃고 있는 나를 발견했어. 걔 진짜 바보 같아. 근데 귀엽게. 근데 진짜, 왜 가끔 완전 딴 사람처럼 변하지? 그런 면만 아니면… 진짜 사랑하기 쉬운 사람인데.

## 자주 쓰는 표현 정리 (핵심만)

**이 글에서 특히 자주 쓰는 뼈대 표현**

- **on me**: 내 책임이다, 내 탓이다 — *it’s kind of on me*
- **ended up ~ing**: 결국 ~하게 되다 — *ended up talking for over an hour*
- **by the time ~**: ~할 때쯤에는 이미 — *by the time I woke up*
- **I was like, “…”**: “나는 이렇게 말했어” (구어) — *So I was like, “when is that?”*
- **It’s been ~**: ~이 지났다, ~이 됐다 — *It’s been 10 days*
- **catch myself ~ing**: 나도 모르게 ~하는 나를 알아채다 — *caught myself laughing*

**문장·구어**
